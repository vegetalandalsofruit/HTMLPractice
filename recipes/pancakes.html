Pancakes that WILL kill you

Ingredients

  • Milk - 6dl/2,5 cups
  • Flour - 3dl/1,5 cups
  • Eggs - 3
  • Salt - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Vanilla sugar/extract - to taste, DO NOT OVERDO IT
  • Butter - Tablespoon for batter, as needed for pan

Note: easily scaled up as long as you keep the ratio 2dl milk - 1dl flour - 1 egg

Preparation

  1. Melt the butter (amount isn't super specific, you can also go without it but it makes flipping slightly easier)
  2. Mix in the sugar, eggs, salt, milk and flour (in this order is the easiest)
  3. Make pan hot but not too hot to not immediately burn the butter, then butter the pan
  4. Pour a thin layer of batter on pan. around 3mm or 1/8"
  5. Let it cook until the top layer is no longer liquid, then flip. both sides should ideally be "lacy"
  6. Keep going until all of the batter is used, enjoy with fresh berries/jam and whipped cream, chocolate/caramel sauce etc.. go wild i don't care

Additional notes:

  • A thin and large spatula is best for flipping
  • Keep an eye on the heat!!!!
