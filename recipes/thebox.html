The Box (of Cabbage)

  • Cabbage (lol) - Around 1kg
  • Water - 5dl
  • Salt - 1 and 1/2 teaspoons
  • Onions - 1 to 2
  • Minced meat (preferably beef) - 800g
  • Allspice - 2 teaspoons
  • Black Pepper - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Dark Syrup - 3 tablespoons
  • Porridge rice - 1/2dl
  • Butter (as needed)

Preparation

  1. Cut up cabbage into thin slices/strips, leave out the hardest middle part
  2. Boil water in a BIG pot (cabbage takes up a ton of space at first). Add salt and cabbage.
  3. Mix (with lid on)
  4. Slice onion(s) and add to the pot, let it boil for 30 minutes.
  5. While the pot is boiling, brown the meat. after the 30 minutes is up, add meat, spices and syrup to the cabbage pot, mix everything well.
  6. Butter up a large cooking dish. add raw rice to the bottom, add the cabbage/meat mix into the dish, including the broth. add small clumps of butter on top.
  7. Cook at 200C on the bottom of the oven for 50 minutes.

Additional notes:

  • Serve with lingonberry jam if you're feeling lowkey kinda zesty.
